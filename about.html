About FontDale

Discover the story behind Arcadia Circle's most prestigious shopping destination.

The Vision and Founder

FontDale is a premium lifestyle mall conceptualized in 2023 by visionary designer and entrepreneur Franz Diego. Opened to the public in 2025, FontDale was built with a clear purpose: to redefine the modern urban retail experience. Franz Diego envisioned a space where luxury, culture, and community naturally coexist. Today, the mall stands as a reflection of that original vision, serving as a peaceful retreat and a premier shopping destination in the heart of Arcadia Circle.

The Architectural Concept

At its core, FontDale represents a sophisticated blend of contemporary design, high-quality materials, and immersive experiences. Every detail has been carefully considered, from the curated rose gold accent details to the spacious, light-filled galleries. The mall is architecturally organized into two distinct wings, the North Gallery and the South Pavilion, which are seamlessly connected by the signature Lumen Skywalk. This cohesive design ensures that every visit feels effortless, aesthetic, and memorable.

North Gallery

The North Gallery is the heart of high fashion and luxury at FontDale. Spanning across elegantly designed corridors, this wing hosts premier global fashion brands, fine jewelry boutiques, and exclusive lifestyle stores. The gallery is characterized by its sleek, minimalist aesthetic, featuring grand high ceilings and wide storefronts that allow visitors to explore the absolute best of luxury retail at their own pace.

South Pavilion

The South Pavilion is dedicated to culinary experiences, social life, and wellness. It features a diverse range of artisanal cafes, fine dining establishments, and high-end spa retreats. The architectural theme here is warm and organic, utilizing natural textures to create an inviting space where visitors can gather, unwind, and enjoy curated experiences. The pavilion is home to beloved spaces like Café Rosetta and Spa Serenity, making it the perfect setting for leisure and relaxation.

Lumen Skywalk

Connecting the two main wings is the breathtaking Lumen Skywalk. This architectural highlight is a glass-enclosed walkway that offers stunning panoramic views of Arcadia Circle. Designed to be bathed in natural sunlight by day and softly illuminated by night, the skywalk serves as a peaceful transition zone that turns a simple walk between galleries into a scenic, memorable experience.
